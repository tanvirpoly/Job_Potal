MYJOBS - JOB PLACEMENT CENTER

MYJOBS is a responsive -reliable JOB PLACEMENT & CONSULTANCY firm in Bangladesh -providing employment & consultancy services towards hundreds of local and Multinational companies at any level.

Beside the regular free Job Portal service MYJOBS trying to extend the service towards candidates who are actively & eagerly looking for a quick employment. To ensure the quality employment of the candidate MYJOBS offers 7 required services which comes under a paid PRIORITY MEMBERSHIP service. And for a successful employment we will also charge for 'PLACEMNET Service' where candidates need to agree & sign a form to pay a service charge.

Through the systematic assessment process we will groom candidates for wining interview successfully. Our long experienced professional recruiter can easily identify strength and weakness of the candidate and they can guide you to be prepared for any aspired job. You can find the ultimate career guideline from industry experts according to your competency and interest to be successful in professional and personal life.
