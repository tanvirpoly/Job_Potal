COMPENSATION OVERVIEW

Compensation is the comprehensive package of rewards and benefits that employees receive for their work. It encompasses their base salary or wages, performance-related bonuses, and non-monetary perks like health insurance, retirement plans, and stock options. Some may earn commissions or profit-sharing, while others enjoy company perks and incentives. Compensation plays a crucial role in attracting and retaining talent, motivating employees, and ensuring they are fairly rewarded for their contributions, ultimately contributing to a company's success and employee satisfaction.
